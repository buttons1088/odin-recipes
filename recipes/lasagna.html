Lasagna

Description

A tower of mince meat, pasta sheets, white sauce, and tasty cheese topping.

If you want to go the extra mile, blend crackers and Parmiagiano Reggiano to sprinkle on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion (finely diced)
  • 3/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder (or 5 cloves, finely minced)
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 32-40 ounces har Marinara Sauce

Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling

  • 2 cups Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Basil
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 cups Mozzarella Cheese

Instructions

  1. Prehead oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add ground beed and increase heat to medium. Cook until browned (about 5 mins)
  4. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  5. Lower heat and add marinara sauce. Allow sauce to simmer.

Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling

  1. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook lasagne noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Spread bottom of a 9 x 13 baking pan with 1/4 cup of the meat sauce.
  4. Place 3 noodles in a single layer on top of the sauce, and spread with the Parmesan filling.
  5. Sprinkle with 1 cup mozzarella
  6. Repeat to use up all meat sauce and Ricotta Cheese Filling.
  7. Top lasagna with left over mozzarella.
  8. Spray foil with cooking spray and cover lasagna.
  9. Bake for 15 mins and remove the foil.
  10. Continue to bake until cheese is bubbling, about 25 minutes longer.
  11. Remove from oven and cool for 10 minutes.
  12. Cut into pieces, serve, and enjoy.
