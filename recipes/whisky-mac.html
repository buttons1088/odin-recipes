Whisky Mac Cocktail Recipe

Description

Get the weekend started right with this simple cocktail, taking ginger wine's heat and pairing it with scotch.

We recommend serving on the rocks, but feel free to serve without ice if that's your preference.

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 25ml ginger wine
  • 40ml blended scotch
  • strip of pared lemon zest, to serve

Method

  1. Put a few ice cubes in a rocks glass.
  2. Pour over the ginger wine and scotch.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Serve with a strip of freshly pared lemon zest
  5. Drink up!
